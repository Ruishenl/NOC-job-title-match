Operate multi-function process control machinery through control panels, computer terminals or other control systems to mix and melt raw materials; heat, anneal, temper or form float glass or glass products; and coat glass with silver or other metals and materials. Observe gauges, computer printouts and video monitors to verify specified processing conditions and make adjustments as necessary. Maintain shift log of production and other data. May operate process control machinery to cut glass or assemble glass window units. May clean and maintain process machinery. May ensure adequate levels of oil, soap, talc or other materials used in machine production of glass.
