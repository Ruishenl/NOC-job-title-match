Assist in the development of architectural designs. Analyze building codes, by-laws, space requirements, site requirements and other technical documents and reports. Prepare manual and CAD (computer-assisted design) drawings, specifications, cost estimates and listings of quantities of material from conceptual drawings and instructions. Construct architectural and display models, and 3-D virtual models of architectural designs. Prepare contract and bidding documents. May supervise drafters, technicians and technologists on the architectural team. May supervise construction projects and co-ordinate, monitor and inspect work done by others.
