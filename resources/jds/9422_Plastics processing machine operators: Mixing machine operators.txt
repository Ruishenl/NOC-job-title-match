Weigh resins, colorants and other chemicals according to formula. Place weighed materials in mixing machine and operate machine to blend plastic and other chemicals to a specified consistency or viscosity. Monitor operation, regulate material supply and add chemicals and colorants to mixture. Operate controls to regulate temperature, speed and other processing conditions. Unload mixture into container or conveyor for further processing. Maintain and perform minor repairs on machinery as required. Maintain inventory of material and clean working area.
