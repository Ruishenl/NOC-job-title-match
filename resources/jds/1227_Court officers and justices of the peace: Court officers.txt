Co-ordinate administrative services and establish work priorities for court staff. Schedule court trials and arrange pre-trial conferences and hearings. Call courts of law to order, read charges and take pleas from defendants. Record court commencement, trial proceedings and judgements. Collect and record sheriff fees, transcription fees and other court administrative and services fees. Oversee the maintenance of judicial court records. Assist in preparing annual budgets. May be justices of the peace.
