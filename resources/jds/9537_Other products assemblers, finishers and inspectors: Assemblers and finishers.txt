Operate machines or use hand tools to cut, shape and fit materials to form parts and to fabricate components or products. Load supplies into hoppers of dispensing units or manually align, feed or otherwise place materials into machines. Screw, clip, glue, bond, weld or otherwise assemble parts and components to form final products. Sand, trim, grind or clean products into final form using hand tools. Clean work stations and remove scraps, dust, shavings or other by-products from machinery and surrounding areas. May make routine changes of dies, nozzles or other machinery attachments as required.
