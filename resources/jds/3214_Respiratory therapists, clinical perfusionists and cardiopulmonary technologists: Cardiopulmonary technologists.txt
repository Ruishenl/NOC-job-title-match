Perform diagnostic tests, such as pulmonary function and asthma stress, or assist physicians with cardiac and cardiopulmonary stress tests and bronchoscopies. Determine patients' blood characteristics such as activated clotting time and oxygen saturation. Operate, monitor, maintain, calibrate and test diagnostic and therapeutic equipment. Monitor patients and advise physician of any changes in patients' condition. Prepare medications and administer inhaler and other treatments under supervision of cardiologist. Provide information and care for patients during tests. Assist with the preparation of cardiac catheterization room, prepare specialized catheters and assist cardiologists during catheterization. Perform analysis, programming and monitoring of implanted devices such as pacemakers and defibrillators during surgery. Supervise and train students and other cardiopulmonary technologists. Provide technical support for research.
