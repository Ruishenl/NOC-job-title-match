Review job orders to determine job specifications such as production time, colour sequence and quantities required, and advise press crew of these specifications. Mount plates or cylinders and make necessary adjustments. Fill ink fountains and take measurements, make adjustments and determine settings to control colour and viscosity. Set up press and check samples for ink coverage, alignment and registration. Monitor regular press runs for quality and consistency using computer control console and make adjustments as required. Remove and clean plates and cylinders at end of press run. Direct activities of press crew and ensure that safety procedures are adhered to.
