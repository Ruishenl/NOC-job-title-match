Recommend acquisition of books, periodicals and audio-visual, interactive media and other materials for inclusion in library collection. Provide reference services. Select, classify, catalogue and weed library materials. Prepare bibliographies, indexes, reading lists, guides and other finding aids. Develop systems to access library collections. Perform manual, on-line and interactive media reference searches to assist users in accessing library materials and arrange for interlibrary loans. Develop taxonomies using various information and data sources. Provide specialized programs for children, seniors and other groups. Conduct library information and orientation training programs and tours. Perform related administrative duties and supervise library technicians, assistants and clerks.
