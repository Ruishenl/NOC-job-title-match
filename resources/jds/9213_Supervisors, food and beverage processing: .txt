Supervise, co-ordinate and schedule the activities of workers who process, package, test and grade food and beverage products. Establish methods to meet work schedules and co-ordinate work activities with other departments. Resolve work problems and recommend measures to improve productivity and product quality. Requisition materials and supplies. Train staff in job duties, safety procedures and company policy. Recommend personnel actions such as hirings and promotions. Prepare production and other reports.
