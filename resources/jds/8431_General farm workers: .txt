Plant, fertilize, cultivate, spray, irrigate and harvest crops. Feed and tend livestock and poultry. Milk cows. Perform or assist in performing breeding activities of farm animals. Operate and maintain farm machinery and equipment. Follow food safety and security and animal bio-security procedures. Detect disease and health problems in crops, livestock and poultry.
