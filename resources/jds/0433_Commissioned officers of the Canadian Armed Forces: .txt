Plan, organize and command the activities of Canadian Armed Forces combat units such as armour, artillery and infantry battalions. Develop and implement military procedures and policies based on detailed knowledge of units' capabilities and performance. Direct subordinates in training and co-ordinate and direct activities of units in accordance with military practices. Assume responsibility for welfare, morale and discipline of units. Review and evaluate unit performance, prepare reports and provide briefings for superiors. May command and lead units engaged in non-combat operations for disaster relief and humanitarian and peacekeeping efforts domestically and internationally.
