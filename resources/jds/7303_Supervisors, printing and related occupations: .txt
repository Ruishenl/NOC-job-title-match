Supervise, co-ordinate and schedule activities of workers who produce camera work and printing plates and cylinders; process film; and print, bind and finish books, newspapers, business forms and other printed products. Establish methods to meet work schedules and co-ordinate work activities with other departments. Review and approve all job proofs or samples in order to ensure quality of work meets client specifications. Requisition materials and supplies. Resolve work problems, provide technical advice and recommend measures to improve productivity and product quality. Train staff in job duties, safety procedures and company policies. Recommend personnel actions such as hiring and promotions. Prepare production and other reports. May set up machines or equipment.
