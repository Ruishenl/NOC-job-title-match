Design, fabricate and fit prosthetic and orthotic appliances such as artificial limbs, braces or supports. Interpret physicians' specifications and examine and measure patients to develop working sketches of appliances. Make and modify plaster casts of areas to receive prostheses or orthoses. Fit, adjust and repair appliances. Advise patients in the use and care of prostheses and orthoses. Supervise and direct the activities of prosthetic and orthotic technicians.
