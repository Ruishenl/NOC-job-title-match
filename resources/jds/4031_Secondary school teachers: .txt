Prepare subject material for presentation to students according to an approved curriculum. Teach students using a systematic plan of lectures, discussions, audio-visual presentations, and laboratory, shop and field studies. Assign and correct homework. Prepare, administer and correct tests. Evaluate progress, determine individual needs of students and discuss results with parents and school officials. Prepare and implement remedial programs for students requiring extra help. Participate in staff meetings, educational conferences and teacher training workshops. May teach and evaluate students through distance or online courses. May advise students on course selection and on vocational and personal matters. May supervise student teachers.
