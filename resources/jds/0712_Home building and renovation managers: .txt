Plan, organize, direct, control and evaluate the activities of a residential home building or renovating company. Prepare bids for home construction or renovation projects. Consult with customers, architects and engineers regarding plans and specifications. Select, hire and supervise trade subcontractors to do specialized work, such as plumbing, heating and electrical work. Plan and prepare work schedules and co-ordinate the activities of subcontractors. Inspect work performed by subcontractors to ensure quality and conformity with plans and specifications. Prepare and maintain a directory of suppliers and trade subcontractors. Develop and implement marketing and advertising strategies to promote company's products and services. May perform carpentry or other trade work during home construction or renovation activities.
