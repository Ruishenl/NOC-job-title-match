Supervise, co-ordinate and schedule activities of workers who assemble and inspect products such as aircraft, buses, heavy trucks, transmissions, automobile engines and refrigeration and heating equipment. Establish methods to meet work schedules and co-ordinate work activities with other departments. Resolve work problems and recommend measures to improve productivity and product quality. Requisition materials and supplies. Train staff in job duties, safety procedures and company policies. Recommend personnel actions such as hirings and promotions. Prepare production and other reports. May set up machines and equipment.
