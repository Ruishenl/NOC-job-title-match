Supervise, co-ordinate and schedule the activities of production workers who operate machines to process natural, synthetic and hybrid textile fibre, yarn and thread; bleach, dye and finish textiles; operate manufacturing machines to cut, stitch and produce fabric, fur or leather garments and other products; and inspect textile products. Establish methods to meet work schedules and co-ordinate work activities with other departments. Resolve work problems and recommend measures to improve productivity and product quality. Requisition materials and supplies. Train staff in job duties, safety procedures and company policies. Recommend personnel actions such as hirings and promotions. Prepare production and other reports. May set up machines and equipment.
