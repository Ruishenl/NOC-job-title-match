Inspect buildings and outside areas at regular intervals or at specific request of property owner to detect signs of infestation. Determine type of treatment required and provide estimate of costs to clients. Prepare and spray chemical mixture on infested areas using mechanical or electric sprayer to kill insects, rodents and other pests. Fumigate households. Place and set traps to capture and remove animals, such as skunks or raccoons. Install animal control barriers such as needle strips, netting and other devices to keep animals off property. Clean out areas that harbour pests using rakes, shovels, brooms or mops. Advise clients on how to prevent pest infestation.
