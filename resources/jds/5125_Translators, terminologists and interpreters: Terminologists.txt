Interpret oral communication from one language to another aloud or using electronic equipment, either simultaneously (as the speaker speaks), consecutively (after the speaker speaks) or whispered (speaking in a low whisper to one or two persons as the speaker is talking). Provide interpretation services in court or before administrative tribunals. May interpret language for individuals and small groups travelling in Canada and abroad. May interpret for persons speaking an Aboriginal or foreign language in a variety of circumstances. May train other interpreters.
