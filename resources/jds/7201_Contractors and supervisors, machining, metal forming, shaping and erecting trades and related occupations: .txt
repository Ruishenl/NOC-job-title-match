Supervise, co-ordinate and schedule the activities of workers who shape, form and join metal parts or products to specified dimensions, machine metal into parts, products, tools and dies or moulds with precise measurements and erect light and heavy metal products and structures. Establish methods to meet work schedules and co-ordinate work activities with other departments. Requisition materials and supplies. Resolve work problems and recommend work measures to improve productivity. Train or arrange for training of workers. Recommend personnel actions such as hirings and promotions. Ensure standards for safe working conditions are observed. Prepare production and other reports. Set up machines and equipment and write programs for computer numerically controlled (CNC) machine tools. May manage the operations of their own companies. May also supervise, co-ordinate and schedule the activities of related apprentices, machine operators, helpers and labourers.
