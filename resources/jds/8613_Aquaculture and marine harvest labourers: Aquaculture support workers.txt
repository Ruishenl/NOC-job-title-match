Assist aquaculture technicians in the operation of fish hatcheries or other aquatic farms. Feed aquaculture stocks, vaccinate stocks, perform culling and marking or banding techniques and report any observed irregularities in stocks. Operate, maintain and clean pumps, filters, tanks and other aquaculture equipment and clean and maintain aquaculture enclosures. Keep daily records of water flow and fish, shellfish and marine plant samples. Grade and weigh aquaculture stocks. Prepare aquaculture stocks for market. May operate boats for aquaculture operations.
