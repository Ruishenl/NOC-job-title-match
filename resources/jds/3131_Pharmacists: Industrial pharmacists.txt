Participate in research for the development of new drugs. Formulate new drug products developed by medical researchers. Test new drug products for stability and to determine absorption and elimination patterns. Co-ordinate clinical investigations of new drugs. Control the quality of drug products during production to ensure that they meet standards of potency, purity, uniformity, stability and safety. Develop information materials concerning the uses, properties and risks of particular drugs. Evaluate labelling, packaging and advertising of drug products. Promote pharmaceutical products to health professionals.
