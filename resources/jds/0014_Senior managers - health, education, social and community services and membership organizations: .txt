Establish objectives for the organization or institution and formulate or approve policies and programs. Authorize and organize the establishment of major departments and associated senior staff positions. Allocate material, human and financial resources to implement organizational policies and programs; establish financial and administrative controls; formulate and approve promotional campaigns; and approve overall personnel planning. Select middle managers, directors or other executive staff. Co-ordinate the work of regions, divisions or departments. Represent the organization, or delegate representatives to act on behalf of the organization, in negotiations or other official functions.
