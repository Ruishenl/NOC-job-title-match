Supervise, co-ordinate and schedule the activities of construction workers engaged in bricklaying, roofing, cement finishing, tilesetting, plastering, drywall installation, glazing, insulating and painting, and workers who install and service prefabricated products in residential and commercial properties, as well as workers who repair a wide variety of products, such as musical instruments, sports equipment, vending machines, bicycles and cameras. Establish methods to meet work schedules and co-ordinate work activities with other subcontractors. Resolve work problems and recommend measures to improve productivity and product quality. Requisition materials and supplies. Train workers in job duties, safety procedures and company policies. Recommend personnel actions such as hirings and promotions. Prepare work progress reports. May manage the operations of own company. May also supervise, co-ordinate and schedule the activities of related apprentices, helpers and labourers.
