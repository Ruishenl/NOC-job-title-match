Develop policies and procedures for managing current and semicurrent archives, in particular corporate cataloguing systems, records scheduling and disposal, and finding aids. Design programs for managing, disseminating and storing archives of all types (documents, photographs, maps, audio-visual materials, manuscripts, etc.). Plan the computerized management of archives and the management of electronic archives. Appraise and acquire archival materials to build and develop an archival collection for research purposes. Authenticate documents and records, and research the origins and significance of archival materials. Organize noncurrent archives and develop cataloguing and retrieval systems to allow access to archival materials. Assist people with their searches.
