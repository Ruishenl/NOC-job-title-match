Plan, organize, direct, control and evaluate, through department heads or supervisors, the activities of teachers and support staff. Review programs to ensure conformance to school board or provincial standards and develop programs within limits of authority. Co-ordinate teaching activities of the institution by making personnel assignments, determining class size and preparing timetables. Organize and maintain procedures for the keeping of records. Prepare and administer institution or program budget. Direct and co-ordinate school maintenance services and the use of school facilities. May recruit and hire teachers and other staff. May teach.
