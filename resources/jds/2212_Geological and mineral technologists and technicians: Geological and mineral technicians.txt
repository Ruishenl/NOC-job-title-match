Participate in geological, geophysical, geochemical, hydrographic or oceanographic surveys, prospecting field trips, exploratory drilling, well logging or underground mine survey programs and in environmental audits and related environmental protection activities. Operate and maintain geophysical survey and well logging instruments and equipment. Prepare notes, sketches, geological maps and cross sections. Assist in preparing, transcribing or analyzing seismic, gravimetric, well log or other geophysical and survey data. Assist in the preparation of rock, mineral or metal samples and in conducting physical and chemical laboratory tests. Assist in hydrogeological field and laboratory studies and in preparation of reports. Carry out a limited range of other technical functions in support of geology, geophysics and petroleum and mining engineering.
